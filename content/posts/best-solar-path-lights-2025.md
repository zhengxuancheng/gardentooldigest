## FAQ

### Do solar lights work in winter?

Yes, but with reduced runtime. Shorter days and lower sun angles mean 4–6 hours instead of 8–10. Snow covering the panel kills output completely — brush snow off after storms. In regions with heavy winter cloud cover (Pacific Northwest, UK), solar lights struggle from November to February.

### How long do solar path lights last?

LEDs: 50,000 hours (10+ years of nightly use). Batteries: 1–2 years for NiMH, 3–5 years for LiFePO4. Housing: plastic fades in 2–3 years, metal lasts indefinitely. Either buy cheap plastic lights and replace them, or invest in metal fixtures with replaceable batteries.
